TuringBox allows anyone to examine the behavior of artificial intelligence algorithms. As an examiner, you can explore the most recent and popular algorithms on the site. To begin your own study, you can launch a box by selecting a dataset, a few AI algorithms, and a metric. You can also make a call to contributors for the development of new AI algorithms.
