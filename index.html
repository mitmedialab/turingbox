An experimental marketplace for the production and study of AI systems.

The lack of incentives in both academia and industry has prevented the replication and scalable auditing of emerging AI systems. This has resulted in unexpected biases within, unethical behavior of and unequal access to these systems. TuringBox is a two-sided marketplace that facilitates both the behavioural study of heterogeneous AI systems and the production of new, socially-aware AI systems.

On one side of the market, developers upload existing and novel AI systems to both benchmark their algorithms with respect to performance and fairness and to demonstrate expertise in their community. On the other side, evaluators develop and post machine intelligence tasks designed to understand and characterize algorithmic behavior.
