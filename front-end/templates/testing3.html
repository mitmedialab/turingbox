The Swimwear Model Dataset

The Swimwear Model Dataset consists of over 200 matched pairs of images were scraped from online fashion swimwear catalogs such as Nordstrom, Dillards, Target, and more. Matched pairs consist of two images: one image of a standard-size model and one image of a plus-size model wearing the same swimsuit with similar lighting and poses.
