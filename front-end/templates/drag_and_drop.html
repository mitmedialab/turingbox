Dataset Library:


Stanford Sentiment Treebank of Rotten Tomato Reviews

This dataset includes 10,662 English sentences taken from film reviews on the website rottentomatoes.com. Half of which were considered positive and the other half negative. Each label is extracted from a longer movie review and reflects the writer's over-all intention for this review. More information can be found at: https://stanford.io/2Kk0I7Q.

Amazon Customer Reviews

This dataset consists of approximately 4 million Amazon customer reviews and star ratings. The reviews are mostly in English with a few reviews in other languages such as Spanish. More information can be found at: https://bit.ly/2r48L0x.

Sentiment Strength Twitter Dataset

This dataset consists of 4,218 tweets taken from the larger SentiStrength 2 dataset. All tweets were in English. More information can be found at: https://bit.ly/2JuWd98.
