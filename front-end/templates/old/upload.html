TuringBox convenes a community of contributors, who can upload artificial intelligence systems for the greater good. If you have a new AI system and want to see how it compares to others, you can upload it to TuringBox for benchmarking.

As a contributor, you can also look through the boxes examiners have posted to see which AI systems the community wants to study and score points for AI systems with a lot of interest.
